Typography

Hustler's style guide adopts a sleek and modern design by utilizing a single font. This font is carefully selected to achieve a professional and sophisticated aesthetic, ensuring that the website exudes a consistent and cohesive look throughout.

Satoshi

The primary font is Satoshi. You can download the webfont in the link bellow. Please use the WOFF and WOFF2 format for better performance.
