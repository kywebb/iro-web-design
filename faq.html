Frequently Asked Questions

What sets you apart from other competitors?

I've been studying, creating and developing websites for over six years. I have comprehensive experience in not only web design, but also web development. Many web designers are limited to applications that can only design websites, but I have programming knowledge that lets me add custom code and API integrations to any website with ease.

What would our first meeting look like?

Our first meeting is a one-hour phone call where I gather all information I can to develop a streamlined content strategy plan. We'll cover your Business Model, Target Audience, Website Goals, Design Preferences, Functionality & Features.

Can my website have animations and custom interactions?

Absolutely. The tools I use are specialized for smooth animations, hover effects, and scroll-based interactions. In that case, I would recommend the Advanced or Premium Professional Website.

Can you integrate booking systems, payments, or memberships?

Yes, I can integrate Stripe, Calendly, Memberstack, Airtable, Zapier, and more to automate processes.

What happens after my website goes live?

After the website goes live, I will make sure you are prepared to take over and to know how to update your website. I provide in-depth training on how your team can update the website, design, and more through hands on remote control through your computer. Your team will feel confident and comfortable once this is done. I provide a period of free technical post-launch support depending on your package. I also can provide technical support hourly after the launch if help is still needed.

Which website tier should I purchase?

The Starter Professional Website is perfect for solo individuals. The Advanced Professional Website is ideal for personal brands, while the Premium Professional Website is best for businesses. However, people may choose lower or higher tiers for all sorts of reasons.

Our industry level websites are designed to deliver measurable results and drive growth to your site.
